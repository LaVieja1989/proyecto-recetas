    Ingredients:

    1 head of romaine lettuce
    1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
    1/4 cup of croutons
    1/4 cup of Caesar dressing
    Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

    Wash and dry the romaine lettuce, then chop it into bite-sized pieces.

    In a large bowl, add the chopped lettuce, parmesan cheese, croutons, and Caesar dressing.

    Toss the salad until everything is evenly coated with the dressing.

    Season with salt and pepper to taste.
