    Ingredients:

    3 eggs
    1/4 cup of milk
    Salt and pepper to taste
    1 tbsp. of butter
    Optional fillings: cheese, ham, vegetables

Instructions:

    In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.

    Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

    Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook for 1-2 minutes.

    Add any desired fillings to one half of the omelette.

    Use a spatula to fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings.

    Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the omelette is cooked through and the cheese is melted (if using).
