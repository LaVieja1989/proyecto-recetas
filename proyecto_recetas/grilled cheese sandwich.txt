    Ingredients:

    4 slices of bread
    4 slices of cheddar cheese
    Butter

Instructions:

    Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.

    Butter one side of each slice of bread.

    Place a slice of cheese between two slices of bread, buttered sides facing out.

    Place the sandwich on the pan and cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.

    Flip the sandwich and cook the other side until golden brown.

    Repeat with the remaining slices of bread and cheese.
